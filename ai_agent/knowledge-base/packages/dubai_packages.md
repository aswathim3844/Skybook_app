- Prices are per person, based on double occupancy. Single supplement: +30%.

- Packages include free cancellation up to 45 days before departure (full refund).
- Between 45–15 days: 50% refund.
- Under 15 days: no refund (travel credit issued).
- All inclusions are pre-booked — no vouchers, no queuing.
- SkyNest Loyalty members earn full Miles on flight and hotel portions of packages.
- Package prices are locked at time of booking — no price increases after confirmation.
