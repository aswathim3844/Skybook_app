# Visa Policy: Indian Passport → London (UK)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | UK Standard Visitor Visa |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply online at gov.uk/standard-visitor-visa |
| **Cost** | £115 (up to 6 months) / £400 (up to 2 years) / £822 (up to 5 years) |
| **Processing Time** | 3 weeks standard / Expedited (£150 extra): 5 days |
| **Validity** | Up to 6 months per visit (on standard visa) |

---

## Important Notes

- Biometric appointment required at UK Visa Application Centre
- Must demonstrate sufficient funds and strong ties to home country
- SkyNest hotel and flight confirmation letters accepted as supporting documents
- Multiple-entry visas available: strongly recommended if visiting UK more than once a year
- Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) system launched for some nationalities — does not apply to Indian passport holders yet (2026)
