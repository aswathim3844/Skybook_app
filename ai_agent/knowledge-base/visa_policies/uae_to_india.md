# Visa Policy: UAE Passport → India

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | India e-Visa (eTV — Electronic Travel Authorization) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply online at indianvisaonline.gov.in |
| **Cost** | $25 (30-day single entry) / $44 (30-day double entry) / $44 (1-year multiple) |
| **Processing Time** | 72 hours standard |
| **Validity** | 30 days / 1 year depending on type |

## Important Notes

- UAE passport holders eligible for India e-Visa
- Apply minimum 4 days before arrival
- Available for tourism, business, medical, and conference
- Entry through specific e-Visa enabled airports only (all major Indian airports qualify)
