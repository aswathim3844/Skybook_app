## Important Notes

- UK passport holders get free 180-day tourist entry to the UAE — one of the most generous in the world
- Passport must be valid for at least 6 months
- Can extend stay at GDRFA or AMER centres in UAE
- Cannot work without a work visa/permit
- AMER app used for overstay fines (AED 200/day) — do not overstay
