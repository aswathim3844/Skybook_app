# Visa Policy: UK Passport → Paris (France / Schengen)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | France Short-Stay Visa (Post-Brexit) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at French Consulate in the UK or VFS Global UK |
| **Cost** | €80 (standard) — slightly lower rate as UK nationals |
| **Processing Time** | 15 calendar days |
| **Validity** | Up to 90 days in any 180-day Schengen period |

---

## Important Notes

- Post-Brexit: UK nationals need a Schengen visa to enter France and the EU (since 2021)
- ETIAS (EU Travel Information and Authorisation System) expected to launch — once live, may replace or accompany visa requirement for short stays; check gov.uk for latest
- Biometrics required at VFS Global centre
- 90-day rule applies across ALL Schengen countries combined, not just France
- Required documents: return flights, hotel booking, bank statements, travel insurance (€30,000)
