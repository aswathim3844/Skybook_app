# Visa Policy: Indian Passport → New York (USA)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | US B1/B2 Non-Immigrant Visa (Tourist and Business) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at us.embassy.gov — DS-160 form + embassy interview appointment |
| **Cost** | $185 (non-refundable, regardless of outcome) |
| **Processing Time** | Interview wait times vary: Mumbai/Delhi 2–4 weeks; can be longer |
| **Validity** | Typically 10 years multiple entry; duration of each stay as determined by CBP at entry (usually B2: up to 6 months) |

---

## Important Notes

- DS-160 online application must be completed first
- Consular interview is mandatory for first-time applicants
- ESTA not available to Indian passport holders — B1/B2 visa required
- Common reasons for refusal: inability to demonstrate intent to return to India; insufficient financial evidence
- Strong ties to India (family, job, property) significantly improve approval odds
- US Customs and Border Protection (CBP) determines final entry at the port — visa is not a guaranteed entry
- SkyNest can provide interview preparation guidance and document checklist
