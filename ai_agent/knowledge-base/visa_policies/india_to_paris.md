## Important Notes

- Biometrics required at Visa Application Centre
- Schengen visa allows travel to all 27 Schengen countries
- Required documents: passport (valid 3+ months past return), travel insurance (€30,000 minimum cover), bank statements, confirmed hotel booking, confirmed flight itinerary
- Paris is the #1 Schengen visa destination for Indians — approval rate high with complete documentation
