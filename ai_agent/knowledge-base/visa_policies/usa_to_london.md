# Visa Policy: US Passport → London (UK)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | UK Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-an-eta |
| **Cost** | £10 |
| **Processing Time** | Immediate to 3 days |
| **Validity** | 2 years, multiple entry, 6 months max per visit |

---

## Important Notes

- US nationals are now required to have a UK ETA since 2024
- £10 per person — straightforward online process
- Apply before travel; ETA linked to passport electronically
