## Important Notes

- UK passport holders enjoy 30-day visa-free access to Thailand
- Extendable once (7 days) at Thai immigration for THB 1,900
- For longer stays, apply for 60-day tourist visa at Royal Thai Consulate in London (£30)
- TM30 may be required from your hotel within 24 hours of arrival
