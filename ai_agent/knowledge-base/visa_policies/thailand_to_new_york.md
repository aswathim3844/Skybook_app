# Visa Policy: Thai Passport → New York (USA)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | US B1/B2 Non-Immigrant Visa |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at usembassy.gov — DS-160 + embassy interview in Bangkok |
| **Cost** | $185 (non-refundable) |
| **Processing Time** | 2–4 weeks (interview scheduling) |
| **Validity** | Typically 10 years, multiple entry, 90 days max per visit |

---

## Important Notes

- Thai passport holders are not in the US Visa Waiver Program — B1/B2 visa required
- Embassy interview mandatory
- Strong ties to Thailand and sufficient funds improve approval odds
- Apply well ahead of travel — interview wait times can be long in peak periods
