# SkyNest Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026  
**Last Updated**: March 1, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

SkyNest Airlines Pvt Ltd ("SkyNest", "we", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our website (www.skynest.aero), mobile app, or any SkyNest service.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Full name, date of birth, nationality, passport/ID number (required for bookings)
- Contact information: email address, phone number, mailing address
- Payment information (processed via PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways — we do not store full card numbers)
- Travel preferences, dietary requirements, accessibility needs
- Feedback, reviews, and survey responses

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Device type, operating system, browser, IP address
- App usage patterns, pages visited, session duration
- Location data (when app location permission is granted)
- Cookies and tracking pixels (see Cookie Policy below)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- Partner hotel and car rental booking confirmations
- Visa service provider verifications
- Government watch-list screening data (mandatory for all airlines)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---|---|
| Booking management and flight operations | Contractual necessity |
| Identity verification and fraud prevention | Legal obligation |
| Personalised AI travel recommendations | Legitimate interest |
| Marketing communications (opt-in only) | Consent |
| Analytics to improve services | Legitimate interest |
| Loyalty programme management | Contractual necessity |
| Government and security reporting | Legal obligation |

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share your data with:
- **Airlines and airports**: Mandatory for operations, check-in, immigration
- **Partner hotels and car rental companies**: Only booking-relevant data
- **Payment processors**: Stripe, Razorpay, PayU (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Government authorities**: When legally required (immigration, customs, security screening)
- **AI platform providers**: Anthropic Claude (anonymised/aggregated data only for AI Copilot)

We **never sell** your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Booking records**: 7 years (legal and tax requirements)
- **Passport / ID scans**: Deleted 90 days after travel completion
- **Marketing preferences**: Until you unsubscribe
- **App usage logs**: 24 months rolling window

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Fix inaccurate data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Opt-out**: Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To exercise rights: privacy@skynest.aero

---

## 7. Cookies

We use:
- **Essential cookies**: Required for booking functionality
- **Analytics cookies**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel (you may opt out)
- **Personalisation cookies**: Power AI recommendations (you may opt out)

Manage cookie preferences at: www.skynest.aero/cookie-settings
