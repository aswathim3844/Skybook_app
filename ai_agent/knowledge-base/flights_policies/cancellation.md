# SkyNest Airlines — Cancellation & Change Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026

---

## 1. Flight Changes (Date / Time / Route)

### Economy — Saver Fare
- **Change fee**: $75 per person per sector
- **Fare difference**: Any fare difference at time of rebooking must be paid
- **Changes permitted until**: Up to 4 hours before scheduled departure
- **No same-day changes** permitted on Saver fare

### Economy — Flexi Fare
- **Change fee**: $0 (changes are free)
- **Fare difference**: Must be paid if new fare is higher; difference refunded if new fare is lower
- **Changes permitted until**: Up to 1 hour before scheduled departure

### Business Class
- **Change fee**: $0 on all changes
- **Fare difference**: Applies
- **Same-day changes**: Permitted subject to seat availability

### First Class
- **Change fee**: $0 on all changes; full flexibility with no conditions

---

## 2. Flight Cancellations (Passenger-Initiated)

| Fare / Timing | Refund Amount |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver — 72+ hours before | 50% of base fare (taxes returned in full) |
| Economy Saver — within 72 hours | No refund (travel credit 15% of fare, valid 12 months) |
| Economy Flexi — any time before departure | 100% refund minus $45 processing fee |
| Economy Flexi — within 24 hours of booking | 100% refund, no fee (if departure 7+ days away) |
| Business Class — any time before departure | 100% refund minus $75 processing fee |
| Business Class — within 24 hours of booking | 100% refund, no fee |
| First Class — any time before departure | 100% refund minus $100 processing fee |
| No-show (any fare) | No refund; travel credit per fare rules below |

### No-Show Travel Credits
- Economy Saver no-show: 20% credit of fare paid (valid 12 months)
- Economy Flexi no-show: 30% credit of fare paid (valid 12 months)
- Business no-show: 30% of base fare as SkyNest Miles
- First no-show: 40% of base fare as SkyNest Miles

---

## 3. Airline-Initiated Cancellations and Disruptions

If **SkyNest** cancels, delays 3+ hours, or significantly reschedules:

| Disruption Type | Passenger Entitlement |
|---|---|
| Cancellation | Full refund (all charges) OR free rebook + $300 voucher |
| Delay 3–6 hours | $50 voucher |
| Delay 6+ hours | $150 voucher + lounge access |
| Delay 12+ hours | $300 voucher + hotel accommodation (if overnight delay) |

---

## 4. Special Circumstances (Force Majeure)

In cases of extraordinary circumstances outside SkyNest's control — natural disasters, war, pandemic travel bans, severe weather — SkyNest will offer:
- Free rebooking within 12 months (same origin/destination)
- Travel credit for the full fare value (no expiry within force majeure period)

---

## 5. How to Cancel or Change a Booking

**Online (fastest)**:
1. Go to **My Bookings** at www.skynest.aero or in the SkyNest app
2. Select your booking → **Change** or **Cancel**
3. Follow prompts; refund/credit confirmed within 1 hour

**Customer Care**: 1800-SKY-NEST | care@skynest.aero  
**At airport**: Visit SkyNest check-in counter before check-in closes
