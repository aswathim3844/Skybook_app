# SkyNest Airlines — Baggage Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026

---

## 1. Cabin Baggage (Hand Luggage)

### Economy Class
- **Quantity**: 1 cabin bag + 1 personal item (handbag, laptop bag, or small backpack)
- **Cabin Bag Size**: Max 55 × 40 × 20 cm (including handles and wheels)
- **Cabin Bag Weight**: Max 7 kg
- **Personal Item Size**: Max 35 × 25 × 15 cm
- **Personal Item Weight**: Max 3 kg

### Business Class
- **Quantity**: 2 cabin bags + 1 personal item
- **Each Cabin Bag**: Max 55 × 40 × 20 cm, max 10 kg per bag

### First Class
- **Quantity**: 2 cabin bags + 1 personal item
- **Each Cabin Bag**: Max 55 × 40 × 20 cm, max 12 kg per bag

---

## 2. Checked Baggage Allowance

### Economy — Saver Fare
| Route Type | Free Allowance | Fee for Extra Bag |
|---|---|---|
| Short-haul (< 4 hours) | 0 kg (no free checked bag) | $35 for first bag (up to 23 kg) |
| Medium-haul (4–8 hours) | 23 kg (1 bag) | $45 for second bag (up to 23 kg) |
| Long-haul (8+ hours) | 23 kg (1 bag) | $60 for second bag (up to 23 kg) |

### Economy — Flexi Fare
| Route Type | Free Allowance | Fee for Extra Bag |
|---|---|---|
| Short-haul | 23 kg (1 bag) | $35 for second bag |
| Medium-haul | 30 kg (1 or 2 bags, combined) | $45 for third bag |
| Long-haul | 46 kg (2 bags, up to 23 kg each) | $60 for third bag |

### Business Class
| Route Type | Free Allowance |
|---|---|
| All routes | 2 bags × 32 kg each (64 kg total) |

### First Class
| Route Type | Free Allowance |
|---|---|
| All routes | 3 bags × 32 kg each (96 kg total) |

---

## 3. Excess Baggage Fees

Bags exceeding the free allowance will be charged:
- **Economy (Saver)**: $9 per kg over limit
- **Economy (Flexi)**: $8 per kg over limit
- **Business**: $6 per kg over limit

Maximum weight per individual bag: **32 kg**. Bags over 32 kg must be split into two.

| Wheelchairs and mobility aids | Always free; contact accessibility@skynest.aero 48 hours before flight |

---

## 5. Prohibited Items

The following are **never** permitted in cabin or checked baggage:
- Explosives, fireworks, flares of any kind
- Flammable liquids (gasoline, lighter fluid) beyond travel allowance
- Guns, firearms, or weapons (unless specifically pre-authorised for checked baggage in compliance with local law)
- Lithium batteries over 160 Wh (some over 100 Wh also restricted in checked baggage — check regulations)

Carry-on liquids: 100 ml containers only, in a resealable transparent bag (1 litre capacity).

---

## 6. Lost, Damaged, or Delayed Baggage

- Report at the airport **before leaving the arrivals hall** — claims cannot be accepted after.
- Compensation limit (Montreal Convention): **$1,600 per passenger**.
- Delayed baggage: SkyNest will deliver to your hotel or address at no cost within 24 hours of arrival of your delayed bag.
- For higher-value items, purchase travel insurance with baggage cover before flying.
