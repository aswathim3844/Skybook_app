# SkyNest Airlines — Meal Policy & Loyalty Benefits

---

## Part A: Meal Policy

### Standard Meals by Cabin and Route

| Route Duration | Economy | Business | First Class |
|---|---|---|---|
| < 2 hours | Complimentary snack pack + soft drink | Hot meal + beverages | N/A (First not available on short-haul) |
| 2–5 hours | Complimentary hot meal (1 course) + beverages | 2-course hot meal + wine/spirits | N/A |
| 5–8 hours | Complimentary hot meal (2 courses) + snack + beverages | 3-course meal + wine/champagne + dessert | 4-course gourmet meal + champagne + spirits bar |
| 8+ hours | Complimentary hot meal + mid-flight snack + pre-landing meal + beverages | 4-course gourmet + mid-flight snack + pre-landing + champagne + bar | 5-course chef-prepared meal + cocktail bar + pre-landing breakfast + personalised menu |

### Beverage Policy

**Economy**: Complimentary soft drinks, juices, water, tea/coffee. Alcoholic beverages available for purchase ($8–$12 per drink) on routes 3+ hours.

**Business Class**: Full complimentary bar including wines, champagne, spirits, cocktails, and premium non-alcoholic beverages.

**First Class**: Personal cocktail bar, curated wine list (updated quarterly with SkyNest sommelier), Champagne on demand.

## Part B: SkyNest Miles Loyalty Benefits on Food & Dining

### Earn Miles at Restaurants

SkyNest has partnered with 200+ airport restaurants globally. Dine at a partner restaurant and earn:
- **Blue / Silver**: 2 miles per $1 spent
- **Gold**: 4 miles per $1 spent
- **Platinum / Elite**: 6 miles per $1 spent

Scan your SkyNest Miles card or app QR code at payment.

### Partner Airport Lounges

| Tier | Lounge Access |
|---|---|
| Blue / Silver | Not included (pay per visit: $35 / use 3,500 miles) |
| Gold | Access on long-haul routes (1 guest included) |
| Platinum | Access on all routes (1 guest included) |
| Elite | Unlimited access on all routes (up to 2 guests) |

**Lounges include**: Hot and cold buffet, live cooking stations, espresso bars, cocktail bar, shower facilities, business centre, sleeping pods.

### Pre-Order In-Flight Meal Upgrade

Any passenger may pre-order a premium meal upgrade 48+ hours before departure:
- Economy → Business-grade meal: $28 per meal
- Business → First Class-grade meal (where First Class operates): $55 per meal
- Payment via SkyNest wallet, card, or Miles (2,800 / 5,500 miles respectively)

### Dietary Preference Memory

SkyNest's AI Copilot remembers your meal preferences and automatically applies them to future bookings (opt-in via Settings → Meal Preferences in the app). Gold members and above have this activated by default.
