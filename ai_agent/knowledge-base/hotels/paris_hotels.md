# Hotels in Paris — SkyNest Partner Properties

SkyNest has partnered with 5 carefully selected hotels in Paris, covering every budget tier from backpacker hostels to ultra-luxury palaces. All properties are bookable through SkyNest with the bundle discount applied automatically when combined with a flight.
