# Hotels in Bangkok — SkyNest Partner Properties

SkyNest has partnered with 5 carefully selected hotels in Bangkok, covering every budget tier from backpacker hostels to ultra-luxury palaces. All properties are bookable through SkyNest with the bundle discount applied automatically when combined with a flight.

## Luxury Tier — $300+/night

### Mandarin Oriental Bangkok ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Charoen Krung Road, Chao Phraya Riverfront, 30 km from BKK
- **Price**: $350/night (Superior River View) | $650/night (Suite) | $2,800/night (Oriental Suite)
- **Vibe**: Asia's most legendary hotel since 1876. Where Somerset Maugham and Noël Coward stayed. Private pier, two pools, six restaurants, Thai cooking school — timeless perfection.
- **Facilities**: Le Normandie (2 Michelin stars), Lord Jim's, The Bamboo Bar (jazz), Thai cooking school, two pools, river pier, spa
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary longtail boat river tour (30 min) for SkyNest guests
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.9/5

### Capella Bangkok ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Chao Phraya Riverside, 28 km from BKK
- **Price**: $500/night (River Pool Suite) | $900/night (Residence)
- **Vibe**: Newest ultra-luxury riverfront property. All-pool-suite design (every room has a private pool), stunning river views, highest staff-to-guest ratio in Bangkok.
- **Facilities**: Côte restaurant (French), Phra Nakhon (Thai), all-suite pools, Auriga spa
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary speedboat transfer from BKK airport for Residence suite guests
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.9/5

### The Peninsula Bangkok ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Charoen Nakhon Road (opposite Asiatique), 32 km from BKK
- **Price**: $380/night (Deluxe River) | $750/night (Peninsula Suite)
- **Vibe**: Peninsula's celebrated service on the Chao Phraya. Three-tier pool overlooking the river, legendary Sunday champagne brunch, Thiptara Thai restaurant on the water.
- **Facilities**: Thiptara restaurant, The Lobby (afternoon tea), three-tier pool, spa, Peninsula Limousine service
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary river cruise welcome + late checkout 2pm
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.8/5
