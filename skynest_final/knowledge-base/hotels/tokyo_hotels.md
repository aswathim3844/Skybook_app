# Hotels in Tokyo — SkyNest Partner Properties

SkyNest has partnered with 5 carefully selected hotels in Tokyo, covering every budget tier from backpacker hostels to ultra-luxury palaces. All properties are bookable through SkyNest with the bundle discount applied automatically when combined with a flight.

---

## Luxury Tier — $300+/night

### The Peninsula Tokyo ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Yurakucho, 60 km from NRT
- **Price**: $550/night (Deluxe) | $950/night (Suite) | $4,000/night (Peninsula Suite)
- **Vibe**: Arguably Tokyo's finest hotel. Imperial Palace views, meticulous Peninsula service, Peter restaurant on 24th floor with Michelin accolades, spa with Mount Fuji view bath.
- **Facilities**: Peter (bar/restaurant, 24th floor), The Lobby (afternoon tea, 1st floor), spa, indoor pool, Peninsula Academy experiences
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary Peninsula Academy cultural experience (sake tasting or calligraphy)
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.9/5

### Park Hyatt Tokyo ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Shinjuku (floors 39–52 of a skyscraper), 55 km from NRT
- **Price**: $500/night (Park Twin) | $900/night (Suite)
- **Vibe**: Iconic — made famous as the hotel in 'Lost in Translation'. New York Bar (jazz, 52nd floor), stunning city views, intimate but grand.
- **Facilities**: New York Bar & Grill, Girandole, Kozue (Japanese), 47th floor indoor pool, Peak Spa
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary New York Bar welcome drink and city view breakfast
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.8/5

### Hoshinoya Tokyo ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Otemachi (central Tokyo), 60 km from NRT
- **Price**: $450/night (Standard Machiya Room)
- **Vibe**: Unique ryokan (traditional Japanese inn) in a 17-story tower in central Tokyo. Remove shoes at entrance, wear yukata, attend sunrise yoga, soak in rooftop onsen. The full traditional immersion without leaving Tokyo.
- **Facilities**: Rooftop onsen (hot spring), kaiseki dining, tea ceremony, yukata provided, cultural programming
- **SkyNest Perk**: Tea ceremony experience (1 person complimentary) for SkyNest Gold+ members
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.9/5

## Premium Tier — $150–300/night

### Shinjuku Granbell Hotel ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Shinjuku, walking distance to all stations
- **Price**: $180/night (Standard) | $280/night (Deluxe)
- **Vibe**: Design-forward hotel in the heart of Shinjuku. Art-curated rooms, rooftop bar, convenient to everything. Popular with creatives.
- **Facilities**: Rooftop bar, lounge, art gallery, 24h reception
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary Suica IC card ¥2,000 loaded value for trains
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.5/5

## Budget Tier — Under $80/night

### Khaosan Tokyo Origami ⭐⭐
- **Location**: Asakusa (traditional Tokyo area), 65 km from NRT
- **Price**: $22/night (Dorm) | $65/night (Private)
- **Vibe**: Funky, origami-themed hostel in historic Asakusa. Senso-ji Temple a 5-minute walk. Budget traveller and backpacker favourite.
- **Facilities**: Common room, kitchen, Wi-Fi, luggage storage
- **SkyNest Perk**: Narita Express guide and Tokyo walking map provided
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.3/5
