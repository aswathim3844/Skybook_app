# Hotels in Singapore — SkyNest Partner Properties

SkyNest has partnered with 5 carefully selected hotels in Singapore, covering every budget tier from backpacker hostels to ultra-luxury palaces. All properties are bookable through SkyNest with the bundle discount applied automatically when combined with a flight.

---

## Luxury Tier — $300+/night

### Marina Bay Sands ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Marina Bay, 20 km from SIN
- **Price**: $450/night (Deluxe) | $780/night (Club Room) | $2,200/night (Chairman Suite)
- **Vibe**: THE Singapore icon. Infinity pool on the 57th floor (hotel guests only), 3 towers connected by a sky park. Sands SkyPark is the must-see city view.
- **Facilities**: Infinity pool (57th floor), CÉ LA VI bar/restaurant, 20+ restaurants including Waku Ghin, casino, The Shoppes mall
- **SkyNest Perk**: Pool access lounge pass + priority Sands SkyPark entry
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.8/5

### The Fullerton Hotel Singapore ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Fullerton Square (Singapore River), 20 km from SIN
- **Price**: $420/night (Courtyard Room) | $750/night (Quay Suite)
- **Vibe**: Heritage splendour. Former General Post Office (1928), now an architectural masterpiece on the Singapore River. Colonial grandeur meets contemporary luxury.
- **Facilities**: The Lighthouse restaurant, Post Bar, The Straits Club lounge, outdoor pool, spa
- **SkyNest Perk**: Heritage welcome amenity and early check-in from 10am
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.8/5

### Capella Singapore ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Sentosa Island, 25 km from SIN
- **Price**: $600/night (Manor Room) | $1,200/night (Villa Suite)
- **Vibe**: Secluded resort on Sentosa, away from the city buzz. Colonial colonnade buildings, private pool villas, Forbes Travel Guide 5-star. Hosted US-North Korea summit (2018).
- **Facilities**: Fiamma restaurant, Auriga spa, beach club, two pools, private event spaces
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary return ferry or cable car from VivoCity
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.9/5

## Premium Tier — $150–300/night

### The Amara Hotel Singapore ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Tanjong Pagar (business/foodie district), 22 km from SIN
- **Price**: $180/night (Superior) | $280/night (Amara Suite)
- **Vibe**: Stylish four-star in Singapore's most vibrant dining neighbourhood. Walking distance to Chinatown, hawker centres, and the CBD.
- **Facilities**: Pool, Thanying restaurant (Thai royal cuisine), gym
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary hawker centre guide and MRT card
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.5/5

## Standard Tier — $80–150/night

### Ibis Singapore on Bencoolen ⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: Bencoolen (Little India adjacent), 20 km from SIN
- **Price**: $90/night (Standard)
- **Vibe**: Clean, efficient budget option in the city. Walking distance to Bugis and Orchard Road. Popular with backpackers and budget business travellers.
- **Facilities**: Restaurant, Wi-Fi, laundry, 24h reception
- **SkyNest Perk**: Free city map and MRT pass (2-day)
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.1/5
