# Hotels in New York City — SkyNest Partner Properties

SkyNest has partnered with 6 carefully selected hotels in New York City, covering every budget tier from backpacker hostels to ultra-luxury palaces. All properties are bookable through SkyNest with the bundle discount applied automatically when combined with a flight.

## Luxury Tier — $300+/night

### The Plaza Hotel ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: 5th Avenue at Central Park South, Midtown Manhattan, 25 km from JFK
- **Price**: $650/night (Classic) | $1,200/night (Central Park View Suite) | $30,000/night (Presidential Suite)
- **Vibe**: New York's most iconic hotel since 1907. Eloise's home, Frank Sinatra's haunt, now a National Historic Landmark. Central Park views, the legendary Palm Court afternoon tea, The Plaza Food Hall.
- **Facilities**: The Palm Court (afternoon tea), The Rose Club bar, spa, concierge dedicated floor, private shopping experiences
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary Plaza afternoon tea for SkyNest Business/First passengers
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.8/5

### The NoMad Hotel ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: NoMad district (28th & Broadway), Midtown, 25 km from JFK
- **Price**: $420/night (Deluxe) | $780/night (Parlor Suite)
- **Vibe**: Restaurant-hotel by Daniel Humm and Will Guidara. The NoMad Restaurant was considered one of America's finest. Glamorous, intimate, literary in its curation.
- **Facilities**: NoMad Restaurant and Bar, Library, Rooftop, spa
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary bar credit $50 on arrival
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.7/5

### 1 Hotel Brooklyn Bridge ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Location**: DUMBO Brooklyn, Brooklyn Bridge waterfront, 20 km from JFK
- **Price**: $380/night (Classic) | $700/night (Bridge View Suite)
- **Vibe**: Sustainability-focused luxury with unbeatable Manhattan skyline and Brooklyn Bridge views. Living walls, reclaimed wood, rooftop meadow. Perfect for eco-conscious luxury travellers.
- **Facilities**: Neighbors restaurant, Harriet's rooftop bar (iconic sunset spot), spa, pool, urban farm
- **SkyNest Perk**: Complimentary electric vehicle airport transfer for Suite guests
- **SkyNest Guest Rating**: 4.7/5

## Budget Tier — Under $80/night
