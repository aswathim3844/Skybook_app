# Car Rental Fleet — Singapore | SkyNest Partner Cars

**Partner**: SkyRent Singapore + EcoRide SG  
**Important**: Left-hand traffic. Singapore driving licence or IDP required. ERP (Electronic Road Pricing) charged automatically (peak hours, $0.50–$6 per gantry). Petrol is expensive (~SGD 2.50/litre). COE (Certificate of Entitlement) makes car ownership expensive here — rentals are proportionally higher. Excellent public transport (MRT/Grab) recommended for most travel. Cars best for Malaysia road trips or Sentosa.
