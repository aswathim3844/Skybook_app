# SkyNest Refund Policy

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2026  
**Policy Version**: 4.2

---

## 1. Overview

SkyNest Airlines is committed to fair, transparent refund processing. This policy applies to all tickets purchased directly through SkyNest — via the website, app, or customer care. Tickets purchased through third-party agencies are subject to that agency's refund terms.

---

## 2. Refund Eligibility by Fare Type

### Economy — Flexi Fare
- **Cancellation before departure**: Full refund minus a $45 processing fee
- **Cancellation within 24 hours of booking**: 100% refund, no fee (provided departure is 7+ days away)
- **No-show**: No refund. Travel credit of 20% fare value issued for future SkyNest bookings (valid 12 months)

### Economy — Saver Fare
- **Cancellation 72+ hours before departure**: 50% refund
- **Cancellation within 72 hours of departure**: No refund. Travel credit of 15% fare value
- **No-show**: No refund. No travel credit issued.

### Business Class
- **Cancellation before departure**: Full refund minus $75 processing fee
- **Cancellation within 24 hours of booking**: 100% refund, no fee
- **No-show**: 30% of base fare refunded as SkyNest Miles

### First Class (select routes)
- **Cancellation before departure**: Full refund minus $100 processing fee
- **No-show**: 40% of base fare refunded as SkyNest Miles

---

## 3. Refund Processing Timeline

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit / Debit Card | 7–10 business days |
| Net Banking / UPI | 5–7 business days |
| SkyNest Wallet | Instant (within 2 hours) |
| SkyNest Miles redemption | Instant re-credit |
| Cash / Bank Transfer | 10–15 business days |

---

## 4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are **never** refunded regardless of circumstances:
- Airport taxes and government levies (these are remitted to authorities)
- Fuel surcharges on Saver fares
- Add-on services (seat upgrades purchased separately, in-flight Wi-Fi passes, lounge access purchased independently)
- Visa assistance fee ($15 service charge)

---

## 5. Involuntary Changes (Airline-Initiated)

If SkyNest cancels, delays by more than 3 hours, or materially reschedules your flight:

- **Full refund** of all amounts paid (including taxes, surcharges, and add-ons)
- **OR** free rebooking on next available SkyNest flight to same destination, same cabin
- **Compensation**: $50 voucher for delays of 3–6 hours; $150 voucher for delays of 6+ hours; $300 voucher for cancellations

---

## 6. How to Request a Refund

1. Log in to **My Bookings** on www.skynest.aero or the app
2. Select the booking → click **Cancel & Refund**
3. Choose refund destination (original payment method or SkyNest Wallet)
4. Confirmation email sent within 1 hour

Alternatively, contact Customer Care: care@skynest.aero or 1800-SKY-NEST.

---

## 7. SkyNest Miles Members — Additional Benefits

- **Gold members**: Processing fee waived on all Economy refunds
- **Platinum members**: Processing fee waived on all refunds; no-show policy relaxed (50% refund on Flexi fares)
- **Elite members**: Full refunds on all fare types, no exceptions. Dedicated refund priority queue.
