# SkyNest Miles — Loyalty Programme Guide

---

## Overview

SkyNest Miles is SkyNest's loyalty programme. Every time you fly, stay, or rent with SkyNest, you earn Miles that can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, hotel nights, and exclusive experiences.

**Active Members**: 1.8 million+  
**Programme Launch**: June 2019  
**Miles Validity**: 3 years from date of earning (reset upon any earning activity)

---

## Membership Tiers

| Tier | Annual Miles Required | Colour |
|---|---|---|
| Blue (Entry) | 0 | Blue |
| Silver | 25,000 | Silver |
| Gold | 75,000 | Gold |
| Platinum | 150,000 | Platinum |
| Elite | 300,000 | Black |

---

## Earning Miles

### Flights
| Cabin | Miles per $1 Spent |
|---|---|
| Economy Saver | 5 miles |
| Economy Flexi | 8 miles |
| Business Class | 15 miles |
| First Class | 25 miles |

### Partners
- **Hotels**: 3 miles per $1 spent at partner hotels (booked via SkyNest)
- **Car Rentals**: 4 miles per $1 spent on SkyNest car rentals
- **Packages**: Bonus 500 miles on any holiday package booking
- **SkyNest Credit Card (HDFC, Emirates NBD)**: 2 miles per $1 on all spends; 5 miles per $1 on SkyNest bookings

## Tier Benefits

### Silver
- Priority boarding (Group 2)
- 1 free checked bag upgrade (23kg → 30kg)
- 10% bonus miles on all flights

### Gold
- Priority check-in and boarding (Group 1)
- Lounge access on long-haul routes (1 guest)
- 25% bonus miles on all flights
- Refund processing fee waived

### Platinum
- Guaranteed upgrade waitlist priority
- Lounge access on all routes (1 guest)
- 50% bonus miles on all flights
- Dedicated Platinum customer care line
- No-show policy relaxed (see Refund Policy)

### Elite (Black)
- Complimentary business class upgrade (2x per year, subject to availability)
- Unlimited lounge access (1 guest)
- 100% bonus miles on all flights
- Personal SkyNest Concierge (dedicated phone line, 24/7)
- Full refunds on all fare types
- Exclusive Elite events and destination experiences

---

## Miles Sharing and Transfer

- Members may transfer miles to family members (up to 3 linked accounts) at a rate of 1:0.9 (recipient receives 90% of transferred miles)
- Miles transfer fee: $10 per transaction (waived for Gold and above)

---

## Programme Rules

- Miles are non-refundable, non-transferable outside the programme rules above
- Fraudulent activity results in immediate account termination and forfeiture of all miles
- SkyNest reserves the right to adjust redemption rates with 90 days' notice
