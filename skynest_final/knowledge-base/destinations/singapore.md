# Singapore — SkyNest Destination Guide

## Overview
Singapore is a city-state where gleaming skyscrapers share the skyline with colonial shophouses, where hawker centres serve world-class food for $4, and where the Gardens by the Bay glow neon every night. Safe, clean, efficient, and endlessly surprising.

## Hub Airport
**Singapore Changi Airport (SIN)** — Consistently ranked world's best airport. Jewel Changi with its indoor waterfall is worth visiting even without a flight.

---

## Best Time to Visit
Singapore has a tropical climate with no distinct dry season. Rain can come any time.
| Period | Notes |
|---|---|
| Feb – Apr | Relatively drier; comfortable |
| Jun – Aug | Occasional haze from regional fires; Formula 1 GP in Sep |
| Dec – Jan | Slightly wetter but festive — Christmas lights on Orchard Road |

---

## Must-See Attractions

**Icons**: Marina Bay Sands (infinity pool), Gardens by the Bay (Supertree Grove, Cloud Forest, Flower Dome), Merlion Park
**Culture & Heritage**: Chinatown (Buddha Tooth Relic Temple), Little India (Mustafa Centre), Arab Street (Sultan Mosque), Colonial District (Raffles Hotel, National Museum)
**Nature**: Singapore Botanic Gardens (UNESCO), MacRitchie Reservoir treetop walk, Sentosa Island beaches
**Food**: Maxwell Food Centre, Lau Pa Sat, Newton Food Centre, Hawker Chan (Michelin-starred hawker)
**Shopping**: Orchard Road, Bugis Street, VivoCity, Haji Lane boutiques

---

## Neighbourhoods
- **Marina Bay**: CBD, luxury, iconic skyline
- **Chinatown / Tanjong Pagar**: Heritage and hipster food
- **Orchard Road**: Shopping hub
- **Kampong Glam**: Malay culture, vintage shops
- **East Coast**: Local residential, beaches, seafood

---

## Getting Around
- **MRT (Mass Rapid Transit)**: Extensive, efficient, ~SGD 0.90–2.20 per trip; EZ-Link card recommended
- **Buses**: Comprehensive network; same card as MRT
- **Grab (ride-hailing)**: App-based, reliable, ~SGD 8–20 for most city journeys
- **Walking**: Many areas very walkable; covered walkways between many buildings

---

## Currency
Singapore Dollar (SGD). USD 1 ≈ SGD 1.34 (2026). Cards widely accepted; hawker centres prefer cash.

---

## Practical Tips
- Fines for chewing gum, littering, and jaywalking are real — take them seriously
- Tipping not customary or expected
- Street food is safe and excellent — do not skip hawker centres
- GST (9%) applies to most goods and services; Tourist Refund Scheme available for purchases over SGD 100
