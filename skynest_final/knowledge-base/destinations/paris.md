# Paris — SkyNest Destination Guide

## Overview
Paris is the city of light, love, and art — where the Eiffel Tower glimmers at night, where every arrondissement has a neighbourhood bistro serving perfect steak frites, and where world-class museums could occupy a lifetime of visits. SkyNest connects Delhi, Dubai, Singapore, London, New York, and Bangkok directly to Charles de Gaulle.

## Hub Airport
**Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG)** — Terminal 2E for SkyNest arrivals. RER B train to city centre: 35 minutes, €11.80. Roissybus to Opéra: 60 minutes, €16.10. Taxi to central Paris: ~€55 (fixed flat rate).

## Best Time to Visit
| Season | Months | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Spring (Best) | Apr – Jun | Mild (15–22°C), blooming parks, long days |
| Summer | Jul – Aug | Warm but busy; August locals leave, tourists arrive |
| Autumn | Sep – Nov | Golden light, shorter queues, harvest season |
| Winter | Dec – Jan | Cold but festive; Christmas markets, fewer tourists |

**Fashion Week**: Jan/Feb (Haute Couture), Mar (Womenswear), Jun (Menswear), Oct (Womenswear) — hotels peak during these weeks.

## Must-See Attractions

**Icons**: Eiffel Tower (book timed entry 2+ months ahead), Louvre Museum (Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo), Notre-Dame Cathedral (reopened 2025 after restoration), Arc de Triomphe, Montmartre / Sacré-Cœur
**Art**: Musée d'Orsay (Impressionists), Centre Pompidou (modern art), Musée Rodin, Palais Royal
**Neighbourhoods**: Le Marais (Jewish quarter, boutiques), Saint-Germain-des-Prés (literary cafés), Canal Saint-Martin (hipster), Montmartre (artists, vineyards)
**Food & Drink**: Croissant at any boulangerie (the real test of a neighbourhood), escargot and steak tartare, macarons from Pierre Hermé, fine dining on the Seine (Lapérouse, Le Grand Véfour)
**Day Trips**: Palace of Versailles (30 min by RER), Champagne region (Reims, 1.5 hours TGV), Loire Valley châteaux (2 hours TGV)

## Getting Around
- **Métro**: 16 lines, very efficient; Navigo Liberté+ card recommended for multi-day visits
- **RER Trains**: Suburban rail; connects CDG, Versailles, Disneyland Paris
- **Vélib'**: Bike-share scheme, 1,400 stations city-wide; practical and scenic
- **Walking**: Paris's most romantic and practical option for central sightseeing
- **SkyNest Car Rental**: Available at CDG; note Paris centre driving not recommended (Crit'Air sticker required for most zones)

## Currency
Euro (€). USD 1 ≈ €0.92 (2026). Cards widely accepted; some boulangeries and markets prefer cash.

---

## Practical Tips
- Many attractions require timed-entry booking — never just show up at the Louvre
- Learn a few French phrases; Parisians deeply appreciate the effort
- Lunch menus (formule) at restaurants offer great value: starter + main + dessert for €15–25
- Pickpocketing common around Eiffel Tower and major tourist sites — use front pockets or crossbody bags
