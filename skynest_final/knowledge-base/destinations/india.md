# India — SkyNest Destination Guide

## Overview
India is a subcontinent of extraordinary diversity — ancient temples beside glass towers, spice markets filling narrow alleys, and landscapes ranging from Himalayan peaks to tropical beaches. SkyNest operates out of four Indian gateway cities: Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, and Chennai.

## Top Cities & Regions Accessible via SkyNest Hubs

### Mumbai (Bombay)
The financial and entertainment capital. Home to Bollywood, Marine Drive, the Gateway of India, and world-class restaurants. Base airport: **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International (BOM)**.

### Delhi (New Delhi)
India's capital — a living museum spanning Mughal splendour (Red Fort, Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb) and modern India (Connaught Place, Cyber City Gurgaon). Base airport: **Indira Gandhi International (DEL)**.

### Bangalore (Bengaluru)
India's Silicon Valley — cosmopolitan, green, and tech-forward with a thriving café culture, craft beer scene, and proximity to Mysore and Coorg. Base airport: **Kempegowda International (BLR)**.

### Chennai
Gateway to Tamil Nadu — classical Carnatic music, Dravidian temple architecture, pristine Marina Beach, and the cultural depth of South India. Base airport: **Chennai International (MAA)**.

## Best Time to Visit
| Season | Months | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Winter (Best) | Oct – Feb | Cool, dry across most regions; peak tourist season |
| Spring | Mar – Apr | Pleasant in the hills; hot in plains |
| Monsoon | Jun – Sep | Lush and green; expect heavy rain across the west coast |
| Summer | Apr – Jun | Hot across most of India; hill stations popular |

## Must-See Attractions

**North India**: Taj Mahal (Agra), Varanasi ghats, Jaisalmer desert fort, Rishikesh yoga retreat
**South India**: Meenakshi Temple (Madurai), Hampi ruins, Kerala backwaters, Mysore Palace
**West India**: Mumbai's Elephanta Caves, Goa beaches, Rann of Kutch salt flats
**East India**: Darjeeling tea estates, Sundarbans mangroves, Puri Jagannath Temple

## Getting Around
- **Domestic flights**: IndiGo, Air India, SpiceJet connect all major cities
- **Trains**: Indian Railways — book at irctc.co.in; iconic routes include Delhi–Jaipur, Mumbai–Goa
- **Ride-hailing**: Ola and Uber available in all major cities
- **Auto-rickshaws**: For short distances; negotiate or use meter
