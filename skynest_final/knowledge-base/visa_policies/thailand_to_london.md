# Visa Policy: Thai Passport → London (UK)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | UK Standard Visitor Visa |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at gov.uk or UK Visa Application Centre in Bangkok/Thailand |
| **Cost** | £115 (6 months) / £400 (2 years) / £822 (5 years) |
| **Processing Time** | 3 weeks |
| **Validity** | Up to 6 months per visit |

## Important Notes

- Thai passport holders require UK Standard Visitor Visa
- Biometric appointment at VFS Global Bangkok or Chiang Mai
- Required documents: passport, photo, bank statements, hotel and flight booking, employment letter
