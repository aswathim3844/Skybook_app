# Visa Policy: UAE Passport → London (UK)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | UK Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply online at gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-an-eta |
| **Cost** | £10 |
| **Processing Time** | Immediate to 3 working days |
| **Validity** | 2 years or until passport expiry (whichever is sooner); multiple entry; max 6 months per visit |

---

## Important Notes

- UAE nationals are eligible for UK ETA since late 2023
- ETA does not guarantee entry — border officers have final say
- Can stay up to 6 months as a visitor
- Cannot work, study long-term, or receive public funds on ETA
- Apply before travel — do not wait until airport
