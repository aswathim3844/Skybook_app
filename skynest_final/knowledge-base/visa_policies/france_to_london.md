# Visa Policy: French Passport → London (UK)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | UK Electronic Travel Authorisation (ETA) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply online at gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-an-eta |
| **Cost** | £10 |
| **Processing Time** | Immediate to 3 working days |
| **Validity** | 2 years, multiple entry, max 6 months per visit |

---

## Important Notes

- EU nationals (including French) now require UK ETA post-Brexit
- Apply before travel — cannot get at airport
- ETA not a visa — simpler than a visa but still mandatory
- Cannot work or study long-term in UK on ETA
