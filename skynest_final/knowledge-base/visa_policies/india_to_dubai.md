**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|

| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply online at uaevisa.ae or through SkyNest's visa support |
| **Cost** | AED 350 (30 days single entry) / AED 650 (60 days) |
| **Processing Time** | 3–5 business days |
| **Validity** | 30 or 60 days from date of entry |

## Important Notes

- Indian passport holders cannot obtain visa-on-arrival for UAE
- Multi-entry visas available (AED 850/year)
- Must have confirmed return ticket and hotel booking
- Health insurance required
- Must have minimum AED 3,000 in bank account or equivalent
- SkyNest offers UAE visa application support: visas@skynest.aero
