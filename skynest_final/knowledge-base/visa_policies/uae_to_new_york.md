# Visa Policy: UAE Passport → New York (USA)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | US ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at esta.cbp.dhs.gov |
| **Cost** | $21 |
| **Processing Time** | Immediate to 72 hours |
| **Validity** | 2 years from approval or until passport expiry; multiple entries; max 90 days per visit |

---

## Important Notes

- UAE passport holders are eligible for US ESTA (Visa Waiver Program)
- Must apply before travel — cannot apply at airport
- ESTA does not guarantee entry — CBP has final authority at border
- 90 days maximum per visit; cannot extend or change status
- Must have return/onward ticket
- Register on the official .gov website only — third-party sites charge inflated fees
