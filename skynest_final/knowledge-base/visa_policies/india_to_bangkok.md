# Visa Policy: Indian Passport → Bangkok (Thailand)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | Thailand Visa-on-Arrival (VoA) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Obtain upon arrival at Suvarnabhumi International Airport, Don Mueang, or other international ports |
| **Cost** | THB 2,000 (approximately $55) |
| **Processing Time** | On-arrival; typically 30–60 minutes in queue |
| **Validity** | 15 days from date of entry; single entry only |

---

## Important Notes

- VoA can only be extended once (7 days) at Thai immigration office for THB 1,900
- For stays over 15 days, obtain a 60-day Tourist Visa from the Thai Consulate before travel (THB 1,000 + processing)
- Documents needed at VoA counter: valid passport, photo, proof of onward travel (return ticket), proof of accommodation, THB 10,000 cash or equivalent per person
- Thailand also issues e-Visa for Indians — apply at thaievisa.go.th (60 days, multiple entry options)
- SkyNest VoA document checklist available in the app
