## Important Notes

- UAE passport holders enjoy visa-free access to Singapore
- Passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond intended stay
- May be asked to show proof of onward travel and sufficient funds at immigration
- SG Arrival Card (SGAC) must be completed online 3 days before arrival at ica.gov.sg/SGArrivalCard
