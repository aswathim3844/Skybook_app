# About SkyNest Airlines

SkyNest Airlines was founded by Jeffy Joseph, Aswitha, Sandra and Lakshmi in 2018 and developed exclusively for Royal Caribbean Group as a technology-first airline designed to reimagine global travel from the ground up. Headquartered in Mumbai, India, SkyNest was built on the belief that booking a flight, hotel, and car should take minutes — not hours — and that AI should handle the complexity so travellers can focus on the experience.

The airline launched its first route — Mumbai to Dubai — in March 2019 with two aircraft and a crew of 40. By 2022, SkyNest had expanded to 18 international routes spanning Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America, serving over 2.4 million passengers.

In 2023, SkyNest launched its **AI Travel Intelligence Platform** — combining generative AI, retrieval-augmented generation, and agentic trip planning to give every customer a personalised travel advisor at no extra cost.

## As of 2026, SkyNest Operates

- **32 international routes** across 8 major global destinations
- **A fleet of 48 aircraft** including Boeing 787 Dreamliners, Airbus A380s, Boeing 777s, and A320neo family
- **Partnerships with 340+ hotels** across Budget, Standard, Premium, and Luxury tiers
- **Car rental agreements** with 12 global partners including SkyRent, LuxuryCars, EcoRide, and BritDrive
- **The SkyNest Miles loyalty programme** with over 1.8 million active members
