## Pricing Rules

- **Early Bird Discount**: Book 90+ days in advance — up to 25% off Economy
- **Bundle Discount**: Add hotel + car and save an additional 12% automatically
- **Peak Season Surcharge**: Dec 20–Jan 5 and Jun 15–Aug 31: +$50–$150 per sector
- **Miles Redemption**: SkyNest Miles can offset up to 50% of ticket price (10,000 miles = $100)
- **Bid Upgrade**: Economy passengers can bid for Business upgrade from $199/sector via SkyNest app
