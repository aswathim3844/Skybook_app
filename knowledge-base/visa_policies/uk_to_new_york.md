# Visa Policy: UK Passport → New York (USA)

**Status**: ✅ Visa / Authorisation **Required**

---

## Quick Summary

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Visa Type** | US ESTA (Visa Waiver Program) |
| **Visa Required?** | Yes |
| **How to Apply** | Apply at esta.cbp.dhs.gov |
| **Cost** | $21 |
| **Processing Time** | Immediate to 72 hours |
| **Validity** | 2 years, multiple entry, 90 days max per visit |

---

## Important Notes

- UK passport holders eligible for ESTA / Visa Waiver Program
- Apply online before departure — cannot apply at airport
- ESTA does not guarantee entry at the border
- If previously refused a US visa or had ESTA denied, must apply for B1/B2 visa instead
- Register on official CBP website only
